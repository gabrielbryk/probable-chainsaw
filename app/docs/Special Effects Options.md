# Special Effects Options

## Quick Effects (Low Effort)

### Googly Eyes
- For EEAAO reference
- Stick on/near clue
- Instant recognition

### Themed Decorations
- Small spirals for Junji Ito
- Crystal/gem stickers
- Musical notes

### Colored Paper
- Different color per clue
- Rainbow progression
- Meaning through color

## Medium Effects

### UV Messages
- Hidden text appears with blacklight
- "THE REAL MAGIC IS INVISIBLE"
- Good for mid-hunt surprise

### Heat Reveal
- Lemon juice message
- Appears with warmth
- "SECRETS REVEALED BY FIRE"

### Mirror Writing
- Backwards text needs mirror
- "SEE THINGS DIFFERENTLY"

## Advanced Effects

### Smart Home Integration
- "Hey Google, where's Joy's next clue?"
- Pre-programmed response
- Needs setup time

### Musical Trigger
- Must play/hum melody
- Could use app to verify
- Or trust system

### Multi-part Assembly
- Clues form larger picture
- Pieces throughout hunt
- Final assembly reveals message

## Selection Criteria
- Joy's patience level
- Time to prepare
- Cost/complexity
- Backup plan availability
