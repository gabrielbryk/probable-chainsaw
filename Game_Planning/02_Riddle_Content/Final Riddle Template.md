## Quick Decision Helper

### For This Position in Hunt, Choose:

**Position 1-2** (Building Confidence):
- Use Level 1-2 difficulty
- Clear connection to interest
- Single-step solving
- Example: "Where the spiral never ends"

**Position 3-5** (Peak Engagement):
- Use Level 2-3 difficulty
- Layered meanings OK
- Can require more thought
- Example: "In strokes of ink, the world is remade"

**Position 6-7** (Maintaining Energy):
- Use Level 2-3 difficulty
- Keep it fun, not frustrating
- Consider energy levels
- Example: "Where minds align on the spectrum"

**Position 8+** (Victory Lap):
- Use Level 1-2 difficulty
- Quick win before finale
- Celebrate her interests
- Example: "Your favorite game awaits"

## Copy-Paste Templates

### Simple Structure
"[Where/In/Behind] the [metaphor for object], [action or state]"
Example: "Behind the spiral that never ends, your path continues"

### Poetic Structure
"[Abstract concept], [specific detail], [hidden action]"
Example: "Where infinity coils upon itself, hidden truths wait in darkness"

### Reference Structure
"[Character/concept] [action], [location hint]"
Example: "The goddess paints with light, guarding secrets in plain sight"

### Technical Structure
"[Technical term] [metaphorical description], [location indicator]"
Example: "The perfect fifth above middle C rests in silver silence"

## Final Quality Check

Before writing on nice paper:

**The Five Tests**:
1. **Unique Test**: Only works in our home? ✓
2. **Joy Test**: References her interests? ✓
3. **Smile Test**: Will delight when solved? ✓
4. **Fairness Test**: Solvable without hints? ✓
5. **Location Test**: Leads to one place? ✓

If all five = ✓, your riddle is ready!
