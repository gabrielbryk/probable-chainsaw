## Duration & Timing
- **Opening Letter**: 2-3 minutes to read invitation and set the tone.
- **Each Riddle Solve**: Aim for 3-5 minutes with Level 3 text, longer if using harder variants.
- **Travel Time**: 1-2 minutes to move between spaces without rushing.
- **Finale Reveal**: Reserve 5+ minutes for the emotional payoff and gift.
- **Overall Target**: 30-45 minutes for the full experience; adjust by adding/removing riddles or changing hint cadence.

## Physical Flow Principles
- Avoid backtracking so movement feels natural through the apartment.
- Alternate rooms or zones to create a sense of progression.
- Keep hiding spots accessible but still cleverâ€”nothing should require moving heavy objects.
- Secure placement so clues do not fall, get stuck, or cause damage.
- Consider safety (pets, candles, liquids) when choosing each location.

## Customization Decision Points

### Where to Adjust Difficulty
**Make Easier**:
- Use Level 3 riddle text instead of Level 4-5
- Faster hint progression
- Skip Riddle 7 entirely
- Add extra visual cues to physical clues

**Make Harder**:
- Use Level 5 riddle text
- Slower hint progression
- Include Riddle 7 as mandatory
- Minimal decorative cues on cards

### Where to Shorten Experience
**Quick Version (20-25 min)**:
- Riddles 1, 2, 4, 5, 6 only
- Skip crystals (hardest) and gaming (optional)
- Faster hint timers

**Extended Version (45-60 min)**:
- Include all riddles
- Add Okami riddle
- Slower pacing between clues
- More elaborate finale reveal

### Where to Add Riddles
**Insert Points**:
- **After Riddle 1**: Okami Poster (Japanese art theme continues)
- **After Riddle 2**: Music-related riddle (Matt Maeson/Gambino)
- **After Riddle 5**: Second pop culture riddle (specific movie quote)
- **After Riddle 6**: Slime riddle (tactile/sensory)

## Emergency Pivot Points

### If Joy Gets Stuck

**After Riddle 1**: Can simplify Riddle 2 on the fly
**After Riddle 3**: Can skip directly to Riddle 5 (easier)
**After Riddle 5**: Can skip Riddles 6-7, go to finale

### If Joy is Flying Through

**After quick solve**: Offer harder variant of next riddle
**Mid-hunt**: Add Okami riddle or extend Riddle 5
**Before finale**: Add surprise bonus riddle

### If Time is Running Long

**Skip Riddle 7**: Always optional
**Auto-progress**: After 8-10 minutes on any riddle
**Compress finale**: Shorter reveal, focus on gift

### If Energy is Flagging

**Offer break**: "Want to pause here and continue in a few minutes?"
**Skip ahead**: "This one's tricky - want to move forward?"
**Reframe**: "We're almost there - one or two more and something special awaits..."
