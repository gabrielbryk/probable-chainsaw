# Scavenger Hunt - Core Concept

## Purpose
Create a memorable, personalized birthday experience for Joy that celebrates her unique interests and our relationship through an interactive adventure.

## Design Principles
- **Personal**: Every clue connects to Joy's specific interests
- **Balanced Difficulty**: Mix of easy wins and challenging puzzles
- **Multi-Sensory**: Incorporate different types of engagement
- **Meaningful**: Each element celebrates something about Joy

## Core Mechanics
- 6-8 sequential clues hidden throughout apartment
- Each clue leads to the next location
- Mix of riddles, puzzles, and physical challenges
- Culminates in final reveal/prize

## Time Target
30-45 minutes total experience

## Success Metrics
- Joy feels celebrated and seen
- Appropriate challenge without frustration
- Creates lasting memory
- Fun for both participants
