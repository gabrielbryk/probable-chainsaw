# Backup Hints and Help

## Hint System Design
- Start subtle, increase directness
- Maintain fun, avoid frustration
- Preserve sense of accomplishment

## Progressive Hint Levels

### Level 1: Gentle Nudges
- "Think about what we look at every day..."
- "Remember your favorite possessions..."
- "What have you been practicing lately?"
- "Where do we keep our adventures?"

### Level 2: More Direction
- "Check behind things, not just in front..."
- "Your collections hold secrets..."
- "Musical instruments can hide things..."
- "Board games have boxes..."

### Level 3: Direct Help
- "The spiral tapestry..."
- "Your poetry books..."
- "Near your crystals..."
- "The flute case..."

## Delivery Methods
- Verbal hints from Gabe
- Pre-written hint cards
- "Lifeline" system (3 total)
- Timer-based (hint after X minutes)

## Special Situations

### Complete Stuck
- Walk to general area
- "Hot/cold" guidance
- Point to right room

### Wrong interpretation
- "Think more literally..."
- "Try the other meaning..."
- "What else could that refer to?"

### Technical Issues
- Have backup for smart home
- Physical key for puzzle box
- Regular ink version of UV

## Maintaining Fun
- Celebrate each success
- "You're so close!"
- Make hints feel collaborative
- Never make her feel bad
