# Crafting Plan

## Overview

This document details all physical components that need to be created, including materials, dimensions, printing specs, and assembly instructions.

---

## Master Materials List

### Essential Supplies

**Paper & Cards**:
- Quality cardstock (cream/ivory) - 10 sheets, 8.5x11"
- Envelopes - 8 count, assorted sizes (4x6", 5x7")
- Printer paper (backup) - standard white

**Writing & Printing**:
- Black gel pen (for handwritten elements)
- Color printer access (or professional printing service)
- Optional: Calligraphy pen for opening letter

**Assembly & Protection**:
- Double-sided tape (strong hold for tapestry)
- Scotch tape (general purpose)
- Waterproof ziploc bags (small, for crystal riddle)
- Glue stick (for decorative elements)

**Decorative Elements** (Optional):
- Ribbon (purple, silver, black - 3 yards each)
- Wax seal kit with mysterious/elegant design
- Themed stickers (spirals, stars, music notes)
- Googly eyes (pack of 20-30 for EEAAO riddle)

**Finale Components**:
- Special box or decorated container for final reveal
- Tissue paper or decorative filler
- Birthday card or letter (final message)

### Budget Breakdown
- **Essentials**: $25-35
- **Optional decorative**: +$20-30
- **Finale box/gift**: +$30-50
- **Total**: $75-115

## Component-by-Component Build

### 1. Opening Letter
**Purpose**: Invitation to begin the adventure

**Materials**:
- 1 sheet quality cardstock (full size)
- Optional: Wax seal for envelope
- Optional: Ribbon tie

**Specifications**:
- Size: Full page (8.5x11") or folded half-page
- Format: Handwritten or hybrid (printed base, handwritten signature)
- Envelope: 5x7" with decorative closure

**Content** (from [[Starting Letter Options]]):
- Warm greeting
- Adventure invitation
- First hint/direction toward Riddle 1
- Personal touch (inside joke, nickname, love note element)

**Crafting Steps**:
1. Choose letter variant from Starting Letter Options
2. Print or handwrite on cardstock
3. Fold if using half-page format
4. Place in envelope
5. Seal with wax or decorative sticker
6. Place where Joy will discover it (pillow, coffee maker, etc.)

---

### 2. Riddle 1 Card - Junji Ito Spiral
**Location**: Behind tapestry

**Materials**:
- 1 cardstock sheet (5x7" when cut)
- Envelope or small sleeve
- Double-sided tape (strong hold)

**Decorative Theme**:
- Spiral design border (printed or hand-drawn)
- Dark purple or black ink
- Optional: Uzumaki imagery

**Content**:
- Riddle text (chosen difficulty level)
- "Next, seek..." transition hint
- Decorative flourishes

**Crafting Steps**:
1. Cut cardstock to 5x7"
2. Print or handwrite riddle text
3. Add spiral border/design elements
4. Place in protective envelope/sleeve
5. Use strong double-sided tape on back of tapestry
6. Test that it won't fall when tapestry moves

**Critical Note**: Must be securely attached but not damage tapestry fabric

---

### 3. Riddle 2 Card - Ocean Vuong Poetry
**Location**: Inside book

**Materials**:
- 1 cardstock bookmark (2x6" or 3x7")
- Or folded card (3x5")

**Decorative Theme**:
- Poetic, flowing design
- Warm amber/gold tones
- Butterfly or wave imagery (translation metaphor)

**Content**:
- Riddle text
- Transition to next clue

**Crafting Steps**:
1. Cut to bookmark size or foldable card
2. Print/write riddle on one side
3. Optional: Quote from Ocean Vuong on reverse
4. Place in book at meaningful page
5. Ensure it doesn't damage book spine or pages

**Placement Options**:
- As bookmark sticking out slightly
- Fully tucked at specific page number mentioned in riddle
- Inside front or back cover

---

### 4. Riddle 3 Card - Crystal Collection
**Location**: Under/near crystal

**Materials**:
- 1 small card (3x5")
- Waterproof ziploc bag or laminated sleeve
- Optional: Prismatic/holographic decorative element

**Decorative Theme**:
- Crystalline, geometric patterns
- Rainbow/prismatic colors
- Mineral diagram aesthetic

**Content**:
- Riddle text (likely Level 4-5, hardest riddle)
- Transition hint

**Crafting Steps**:
1. Cut small card size (fits under crystal)
2. Print/write riddle with scientific formatting
3. Place in waterproof protection (especially if near opal's water)
4. Position under/beside chosen crystal
5. Ensure crystal won't be knocked over

**Critical Note**: MUST be waterproof if placing near opal container

### 5. Riddle 4 Card - Flute Music
**Location**: Inside flute case

**Materials**:
- 1 cardstock sheet (4x6")
- Protective sleeve (so it doesn't damage instrument)
- Optional: Musical staff border

**Decorative Theme**:
- Musical notation elements
- Silver/pewter color scheme
- Flowing, harmonic design

**Content**:
- Riddle text with music theory elements
- Optional: Actual musical notation
- Transition hint

**Crafting Steps**:
1. Create card sized to fit in flute case
2. Include musical staff or notation design
3. Print/write riddle with proper musical symbols
4. Place in protective sleeve
5. Position in case so it's visible when opened but won't touch instrument

**Placement**: On top of or beside flute, never directly touching silver

## Design Consistency Guidelines

### Color Palette Across All Cards
**Primary**: Deep purple, black, silver, cream
**Accent**: Amber/gold, prismatic rainbow, soft blue
**Finale**: Rich gold, warm white

### Typography Recommendations
**Riddle Text**: Serif font (Garamond, Crimson) or elegant handwriting
**Decorative Elements**: Sans-serif for clarity (Futura, Avenir)
**Finale**: Script or calligraphy for emotional impact

### Visual Motifs to Repeat
- Spirals (callbacks to Riddle 1)
- Stars or constellations (wonder/magic theme)
- Geometric patterns (crystal/precision)
- Flowing lines (music/water)

### Card Size Standards
- **Standard**: 5x7" (most riddles)
- **Bookmark**: 2x6" (poetry riddle)
- **Small**: 3x5" (tight hiding spots)
- **Finale**: 8.5x11" full page or custom box insert

## Printing vs. Handwriting

### Recommendations by Component

**Print**:
- Riddle text with complex formatting (chemical formulas, musical notation)
- Decorative borders and backgrounds
- Anything requiring precise alignment

**Handwrite**:
- Opening letter (or at least signature)
- Final reveal message (most personal)
- Any personal notes or transitions
- Optional: Riddle text if handwriting is clear and beautiful

**Hybrid** (Best Option):
- Print decorative background and borders
- Handwrite the actual riddle text or personal elements
- Print complex symbols, handwrite simple text

- [ ] Place all cards in envelopes/sleeves

- [ ] Gather all completed cards in sequence
- [ ] Double-check finale location is ready

## Quality Control Checklist

For each card, verify:
- [ ] Riddle text is legible and error-free
- [ ] Decorative elements enhance (don't distract)
- [ ] Size fits intended hiding spot
- [ ] Protected from damage (moisture, tearing)
- [ ] Envelope/sleeve closes securely
- [ ] Transitions to next riddle clearly
- [ ] Consistent with overall aesthetic
- [ ] Emotionally appropriate for placement in arc

## Special Techniques (Optional)

### Mirror Writing (Riddle 1 or 2)
- Write part of clue backwards
- Include small hand mirror
- Joy must use mirror to read

### UV Ink Reveal
- Write hidden message in UV ink
- Include small blacklight flashlight
- Visible text hints at hidden message

### Heat Reveal (Lemon Juice)
- Write message in lemon juice on paper
- Clue instructs to "apply heat"
- Iron or lighter reveals text

### Puzzle Assembly
- Cut card into jigsaw pieces
- Joy must assemble to read riddle
- Best for Level 5 difficulty riddles

**Note**: Only use special techniques if they enhance the experience and you have time to test them. Simple, heartfelt often beats complex, gimmicky.
