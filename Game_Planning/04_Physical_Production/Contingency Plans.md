# Contingency Plans

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Clue Problems

#### Can't Find Clue
- Use hot/cold guidance
- Walk to general area
- Give increasingly direct hints
- Last resort: retrieve it yourself

#### Misinterprets Riddle
- "Try thinking about it differently"
- Emphasize key word in riddle
- Redirect to right category
- Provide clarifying hint

#### Clue Damaged/Lost
- Have backup copies ready
- Can verbally give riddle
- Skip to next if needed
- Improvise alternative

### Technical Failures

#### UV Light Dead
- Use regular version of message
- Phone flashlight alternative
- Skip special effect
- Make it verbal clue

#### Smart Home Not Working
- Have written version
- You speak the clue
- Use phone/tablet
- Skip to next method

#### Puzzle Box Stuck
- Have key/combination ready
- Can open it for her
- Transfer contents
- Make it decoration only

### Emotional Management

#### Getting Frustrated
- Take break
- Offer easier hint
- Skip current clue
- Focus on fun not challenge

#### Losing Interest
- Speed up pace
- Skip to favorites
- Go straight to finale
- Make it collaborative

#### Too Easy/Bored
- Add verbal challenges
- Create bonus riddles
- Add time element
- Increase finale surprise

## Flexibility Rules

### Can Always:
- Skip any riddle
- Combine clues
- Give direct help
- Change order

### Never:
- Make her feel bad
- Prioritize plan over fun
- Refuse to help
- Get frustrated yourself

## Backup Entertainment

If Everything Falls Apart:
- Turn into guided tour
- Make it collaborative
- Just do final reveal
- Pivot to other activity

## Remember
The experience and joy matter more than perfect execution.
